Chapter Eleven :

POINT OF NO RETURN


Back to Stardom main hal lwhere for the first time we are intimated that the protagonist's full name is Adebepo who is being given a week long send-off by the school. Purple and black are the schools colours which adorn the main hall of the school. We begin with a farewell match between the school's student team and staff which with a lot of hilarious rigging ends 3-2 to the advantage of the staff. The school's team boasts so much of talents that the keeper, Chidi or Chido had been handed an invitation to the under 17 national team.

Sociocultural activities were held every Friday between 1pm -3pm and it was during this time a dramatic and dance performance was held in his honour. Bepo was so much caught up in one of the dances which reminded him of slavery that he unconsciously blurted a loud ‘No’ to the shock of the hall. But he quickly recovered himself and the situation using his wits. The chapter ends with the MD telling us of how her father, David Aje had employed him and how he had on his first day resumed 8 days late which however was also the last time he was ever tardy.

He was overall appreciated and given at least 10,000 dollars as the chapter closed with a tearful ending.
