Chapter Seven : Ritualists




Bepo used to work at ‘Beesway Group of School’ somewhere on the outskirts of Lagos. The Director of the school, Mr Egi Meko and Bepo were at loggerheads over the name of the school which should have read Beesway Group of Schools, but Mr Meko was adamant to change. He thought Bepo was just an ‘Englisher’ as he was fond to call him. The issue of the school's name was later raised at a PTA which the Director without confirmation. But it was the discovery of the Director as a ritualists along some others that prompted his leaving the schoo in the Ojikwutu environment. Bepo wasn't the best churchgoer, who originally a Catholic was forced by his Pentecostal wife to join her church, Truth Tellers Mission and his characteristic way of affirming the truth was by touching the crucifix.

After his service year in Warri, Delta, he co-established Fruitful Future which flourished for a while and had the incident of Mr Ogo, a parent proposing a ritual to increase the number of pupils. 10 years on, Mr Ogo would be arrested for murder. The chapter ends by raising some of the issues with pursuing a court case in the country.
