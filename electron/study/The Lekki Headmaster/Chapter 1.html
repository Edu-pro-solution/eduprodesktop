Background

The Lekki Headmaster is a prose work written by Kabir Alabi Garba and divided into 12 short chapters. The illustration on the cover features a young man with a backpack walking down a pathway with the iconic Lekki bridge and a building in the background.


Chapter One : Dusk

We are introduced Mr Adebepo Adewale, the Principal of an Stardom School who had been at the school for 24 years is described as acting in an unsual manner which causes some uncertainty amongst his students and staff prompting a lot of conjectures and exaggerations by parents. Members of staff indicated include The V.P., Mrs Grace Apeh, the school nurse, Mrs Titi, Chemistry teacher, Mr Justus Anabel, Mr Oyelana, CRK teacher
Mrs Neke Egbin, Guidance Counselor, while Angel is introduced as the chapel perfect.
The chapter goes on to mention
Mrs Ibidun Gloss, the Managing Director
and continues to the flashback to how the morning actually started whilst also intimating us on the modus operandi of the school and the fact that no one can still confirm what had overcome the Principal.
