Chapter Eight : Missions Unaccomplished


The chapter opens on legal tussle between two families with children named Banky and Tosh. We are made to know that the school encourages a democratic process by election in choosing their prefects. Forms are first obtained after which comes a screening by management. On the Speech Day which was one of the process involved in the selection of prefects, Banky had called Tosh the son of an ex-convict, which led to an uproar. We are made to know that their rivalry went as far as JSS3, and their parents. All these made Bepo feel both families were irreconcilable. The chapter closed on the 5 year old ‘Breath Project’ that he was worried about and how he'd do everything in his power to be available for the launch.
