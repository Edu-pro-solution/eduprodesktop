Chapter Four :

A CASE OF VISA DENIED



Bepo was trying to say his night prayer in his Adeniyi Jones home in Ikeja when Mrs Ignatius' call came in, in the dead of the night complaining of her husband's absence. The book takes us back to Bepo's physical description, complexion and dynamic nature. The story goes back to the Ignatius family as we are informed of how a DNA test reveals that one of their three children, Favour, was not fathered by Mr Ignatius. This was after the whole family had prepared for the relocation. He would later muse on one of his favourite stories, which was the sacking of Mr Ayesoro, the Government teacher. who was sacked or transferred to the property wing of the Stardom organisation,because Bibi Ladele had a nightmare about him.
