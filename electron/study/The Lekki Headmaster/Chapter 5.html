Chapter Five : SNAKE IN THE ROOF



The ‘long break’ was a period Bepo and teachers on duty ensured no dangerous play. The MD on the other hand used the period for rest because of a buttocks pain occasioned by sitting too long, and she had been nursing the pain for 30 years. She decided against her ritual to rest to check out an adjoining land acquired by the school upon which she found to her surprise cars belonging to staff. She was later informed that the Cooperative organisation was their source of loans to finance such. She went out with Jombo gateman to check out the land but to her surprise found out that members of staff had been using it as a parking lot outside the usual school premises. Jeremi Amos the school's Accountant was summoned alongside the principal who informed her that teachers had been able to afford the cars she saw through the school's cooperative loan scheme.
